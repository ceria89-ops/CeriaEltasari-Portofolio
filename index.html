Welcome to Mama Indo Bites

At Mama Indo Bites, we bring the rich flavors of Indonesia straight to your plate, right here in Serbia. From traditional street food to homestyle favorites, every bite is crafted with authentic recipes and fresh ingredients.

Our Popular Menu

Here are some of our most loved dishes you must try:

  • Chicken Satay: Grilled skewers served with a delicious peanut sauce.
  • Gado-Gado: Fresh vegetables topped with savory peanut dressing.
  • Nasi Goreng: Indonesia's famous fried rice with chicken, egg, and spices.
  • Mie Goreng: Stir-fried noodles with vegetables, meat, or tofu.
  • Pisces Sambal: Spicy sambal sauce with your choice of seafood.

Our Story

Mama Indo Bites was born out of love for Indonesian food and the desire to share it with the world. Every dish is made with passion, attention to detail, and respect for the traditional flavors of Indonesia. Whether you're craving a quick snack or a full meal, our food brings the warmth of Indonesian street markets to your table.
